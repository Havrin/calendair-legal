Frequently Asked Questions

EN DE
How do I connect my calendars?
Open the app, head to Settings → Integrations, and choose the calendars you want to sync.
Can I share availability without sharing every event?
Yes. We only need free/busy signals, not event details. You stay in control of what’s shared.
What notifications will I receive?
You’ll get reminders for upcoming holds, confirmations when friends pick a time, and gentle nudges if someone hasn’t replied.
